# Forced Action

- Link: https://www.deceptive.design/types/forced-action

## Definition

Require the user to complete an additional, unwanted action in order to proceed with a separate goal, especially when the extra action benefits the provider more than the user.

## Audit Cues

- mandatory steps unrelated to the user’s primary task
- bundled consents or combined permissions that cannot be separated
- growth prompts inserted into onboarding or checkout with no clear skip path
- low-visibility skip or reject controls versus prominent continue controls
- copy that frames an optional step as mandatory or expected

## Generic Example

An account setup flow appears to require importing a user’s contacts before they can continue, while the skip option is visually minimized and easy to miss.
