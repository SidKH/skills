# Hard To Cancel

- Link: https://www.deceptive.design/types/hard-to-cancel

## Definition

Make signup or subscription activation simple while cancellation is hidden, delayed, dependent on support contact, or materially more difficult.

## Audit Cues

- subscription entry points in-product but cancellation redirected elsewhere
- cancellation flows requiring chat, phone, email, or repeated confirmation without clear need
- account settings with buried or low-visibility cancellation controls
- retention modals, looping offers, or dead-end navigation in cancel flows
- code or copy emphasizing signup simplicity while offloading cancellation to manual support

## Generic Example

Users can start a subscription in a few clicks online, but cancellation routes them through multiple retention screens and ultimately instructs them to contact support.
