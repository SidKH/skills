# Hidden Costs

- Link: https://www.deceptive.design/types/hidden-costs

## Definition

Advertise an attractive initial price and reveal meaningful fees or surcharges only after the user has invested time and effort in the purchase flow.

## Audit Cues

- fees calculated or rendered only at late checkout stages
- low headline price components separated from service, platform, shipping, or processing fees
- logic that withholds total cost until payment review
- copy emphasizing a base price while footnoting significant extras elsewhere
- tests or feature flags that gate fee visibility until late in the funnel

## Generic Example

A listing page promotes a low starting price, but checkout introduces sizable service and processing fees that were not visible earlier in the flow.
